About us

For the love of food, three individuals - Santiago, Savannah, and Fatimah - have come together to develop an application that will revolutionize the way you eat. This App was created to make life easier for users seeking recipe ideas and connects you to the power and joy of food. Instead of eating out or ordering take-out why not save a few dollars and create a amazing meal with the recipe options we prvide. We provide our users a easier way of creating delicious meals by telling us what is inside your refrigerator. This application is based around ingredients that you already have in your kitchen but have no idea what to make of them, input your favorite five ingredients and we will turn your daily items into an enjoyable meal.
