About us

For the love of food these three individuals (Santiago, Savannah, and Fatimah) have come together to develop an app that will revolutionize the way you eat. This is app is based around ingredients that you already have in your kitchen but have no idea what to make of them. This app will create a meal based on the 5 ingredients that you input in the selction process Turn your daily items into an enjoyable meal and save your pockets from buying takeout.
